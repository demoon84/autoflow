# Wiki Lint Checklist

- [ ] Every non-index page is linked from `wiki/index.md`.
- [ ] Every completed-work claim links back to a ticket, verification record, or log.
- [ ] Newer evidence does not contradict older summary pages without a note.
- [ ] Important concepts mentioned in multiple pages have a page or an explicit reason not to.
- [ ] Orphan pages are listed for review.
- [ ] Stale claims are marked with the source date that made them stale.
- [ ] Pages distinguish implemented behavior from planned behavior.
