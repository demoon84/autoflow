- Out of Scope:
  - Do not redesign the Tickets workspace, Kanban/history model, tabs, dialog layer, board data schema, ticket lifecycle, runner controls, wiki, metrics, or navigation.
  - Do not change Korean/English labels except where alignment-related markup requires preserving existing text.
  - Do not modify board files outside this PRD/ticket handoff.
